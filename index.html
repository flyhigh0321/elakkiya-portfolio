Current Focus

Duvalence asks a simple question: how do we know an AI decision was actually right?

Most AI systems are evaluated before deployment, then trusted too quickly in production. Duvalence is being built as an independent correctness layer for high-consequence AI agents: a system that checks completeness, verifies faithfulness against source evidence, applies rules, and only then produces grounded language a human can act on.

01

Completeness

Check that every required field and decision element is present.

02

Faithfulness

Verify that output values can be traced back to real source evidence.

03

Rule Compliance

Apply deterministic, versioned rules rather than relying on model intuition.

04

Grounded Interpretation

Use constrained language generation only after the factual layer has been verified.
